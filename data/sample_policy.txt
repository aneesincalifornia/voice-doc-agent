COMPANY EMPLOYEE HANDBOOK

Section 1: Leave Policy

Annual Leave
Employees are entitled to 20 days of paid annual leave per year. Leave must be taken in agreement with the employee's manager. Annual leave can be carried over to the following year up to a maximum of 5 days.

Sick Leave
Employees can take up to 5 days of paid sick leave per year. Medical documentation may be required for absences exceeding 2 consecutive days.

Parental Leave
Parents are entitled to 12 weeks of parental leave (at 70% salary) for the first child, and 8 weeks for subsequent children.

Public Holidays
The company observes 10 public holidays per year as listed in the company calendar.

Section 2: Work Arrangement

Remote Work Policy
Remote work is allowed up to 2 days per week. Core hours are 10 AM to 3 PM in your local timezone. All remote workers must maintain a secure internet connection.

Flexible Hours
Core hours are 10 AM to 3 PM. Employees can choose their start time between 8 AM and 10 AM, as long as core hours are covered.

Section 3: Health and Wellness

Health Insurance
The company provides comprehensive health insurance for all full-time employees. Coverage includes:
- Hospitalization
- Outpatient care
- Dental coverage (annual limit: $1500)
- Vision coverage (annual limit: $500)
- Mental health services (6 free sessions per year)

Gym Membership
The company subsidizes 50% of gym membership fees, up to $50 per month.

Wellness Program
Employees get access to fitness classes, meditation sessions, and nutrition counseling.

Section 4: Professional Development

Training Budget
Each employee receives $2000 per year for professional development and training courses.

Tuition Reimbursement
The company reimburses up to 80% of tuition for job-related degree programs, up to $5000 per year.

Conference Attendance
Employees are encouraged to attend industry conferences. Approval and budget allocation decided on a case-by-case basis.

Section 5: Compensation

Salary Review
Salary reviews are conducted annually in Q1. Raises are merit-based and market-driven.

Bonus Structure
Year-end bonus (if applicable) is determined based on individual and company performance.

Stock Options
Eligible employees may receive stock options as part of their compensation package.

Section 6: Termination and Severance

Notice Period
- Employees: 2 weeks notice required
- Company: 2 weeks notice for performance issues, 4 weeks for restructuring

Severance
Severance is provided based on years of service:
- Less than 1 year: 0.5 months salary
- 1-3 years: 1 month salary
- 3-5 years: 2 months salary
- 5+ years: 3 months salary

Section 7: Code of Conduct

Attendance
Regular and punctual attendance is expected. Unplanned absences must be reported to your manager as soon as possible.

Workplace Conduct
All employees must maintain professional conduct. Harassment, discrimination, or unethical behavior is strictly prohibited.

Confidentiality
Employees must maintain confidentiality of company proprietary information, client data, and trade secrets.

Use of Company Property
Company property must be used responsibly and only for business purposes.

Section 8: Performance and Reviews

Performance Reviews
Formal performance reviews are conducted annually. Mid-year check-ins may also occur.

Performance Improvement Plans
If performance is below expectations, a performance improvement plan (PIP) may be implemented with a 30-60 day review period.
